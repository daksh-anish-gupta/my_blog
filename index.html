Campus Life



School Chruch

Was Estblished in 1978

Of colonial architecture, St. Xavier's church is a heritage building. Furnished with vintage artefacts and seating, the inside walls of the church are serene. At the altar, is neatly placed the Bible which seems to have guided uncountable souls.



School Library

Was build on 1980

A riot of colors, the library of St. Xavier's school is designed in a fashion to lure kids into exploring the wonder of books. Comfortable seating and ample lighting asures a good reading experience for the students.



School Auditorium

Was built on 1982

Grandeur settles upon the auditorium of St. Xavier's school, with its raised and extended stage to facilitate events visible to the last row of the elaborate seating arrangement.
